<p><p>The automatic capacitor banks market is segmented into three main types: Low Voltage (LV) Capacitor Banks, Medium Voltage (MV) Capacitor Banks, and High Voltage (HV) Capacitor Banks. LV Capacitor Banks are used in smaller applications, typically below 1 kV, to improve power factor in commercial settings. MV Capacitor Banks, ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, are designed for industrial applications, enhancing efficiency in power distribution systems. HV Capacitor Banks, exceeding 35 kV, cater to utility-scale operations, providing reactive power support for transmission networks.</p></p>
