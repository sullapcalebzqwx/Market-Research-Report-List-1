<p><p>The EMT fittings market comprises primarily two types: screw fixed and spinning type. Screw fixed EMT fittings utilize threaded connections that provide a secure and reliable attachment for electrical conduits, ensuring stability and protection against environmental factors. In contrast, spinning type fittings allow for quicker installation by enabling a rotating mechanism that secures the conduit without threading. Both types serve vital roles in electrical installations, offering different advantages in terms of installation speed, security, and adaptability to various applications.</p></p>
