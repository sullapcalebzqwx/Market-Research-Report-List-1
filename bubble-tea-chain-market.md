<p><p>The bubble tea chain market can be categorized into hand-cranking and electric-cranking segments based on preparation methods. Hand-cranking refers to traditional, manual methods where operators actively shake and mix ingredients, emphasizing craftsmanship and engagement. This approach often appeals to niche markets prioritizing authenticity and artisanal quality. In contrast, electric-cranking uses machines for efficient and consistent preparation, catering to high-volume chains aiming for speed and scalability. Both segments address different consumer preferences while contributing to the overall vibrancy of the bubble tea industry.</p></p>
